## The Thesis in One Paragraph

Shift4 earns a 62bps toll on every transaction across 40% of US hotels, 75% of US stadiums, and — after the Global Blue acquisition — luxury retail in 75 countries. Growth is driven by merchant backlog conversion and gateway-to-E2E migration, not macro-dependent consumer spending. Peak leverage of 3.73× in Q1 2026 is the deliberately designed peak — three one-time cash drains hitting simultaneously. By Q4 2026, leverage reaches 2.99× on its own arithmetic. By Q4 2027, 2.00×. The quasi-LBO mechanics amplify equity returns as FCF accumulates above a fixed debt layer. At 10× FY2030E EBITDA on conservative 12% CAGR assumptions: $284/share, 6.67× MOIC, 49% IRR. No multiple expansion required.
